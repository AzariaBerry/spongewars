Episode VIII

THE LAST KRABBY PATTY

The CHUM BUCKET reigns. Having decimated the krabby patty formula. Supreme leader plankton deploys evil robots to seize control of the last krabby patty and therefor the world.

Only General Krab's band of Goofy Goobers stand against the rising tyrant, certain that Fry Cook Spongebob Squarepants will return and restore a spark of hope in the Goofy Goobers

But the Goofy Goobers have been betrayed by none other than, Squidward. As the evil robots speeds towards the krusty krab the brave Goobers mount a desperate escape...
